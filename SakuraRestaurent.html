About Us

History

Sakura Restaurant opened in 2010 and still delights guests with authentic Japanese cuisine. Chef Kazuki Murakami has been working in the kitchen since the age of 17. For over 30 years he has honed his skills in Japan.

Philosophy

In our restaurant we try to show you all the variety of Japanese cuisine and its current trends. We reveal the best taste of ingredients carefully selected by us. Most of the food items are sourced directly from Japan.

Menu

Sushi & Sashimi
Recipe
Make sushi by rolling vinegared rice with fish and veggies in seaweed, and serve sashimi as thinly sliced raw fish with soy sauce and wasabi.
Sushi Rolls
Recipe
Roll vinegared rice, fish or veggies, and wasabi in a nori sheet using a bamboo mat, then slice into bite-sized sushi rolls.
Udon Noodles
Recipe
Boil thick udon noodles, stir-fry or simmer in dashi-based broth with soy sauce, mirin, and your choice of veggies, meat, or tofu.
Salads
Recipe
Make salads with fresh greens, sesame dressing, seaweed, cucumbers, or pickled vegetables for a crisp, tangy flavor.
Ramen
Recipe
Cook wheat noodles in a savory broth made with soy sauce or miso, then top with boiled egg, sliced meat, green onions, vegetables.
Tea
Recipe
Brew green tea by steeping dried green leaves in hot (not boiling) water for 1–3 minutes to preserve its color and flavor.
